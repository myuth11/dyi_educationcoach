üîç Reflection Framework

What? (Description)

Describe what happened during your volunteering experience

  • Key events and activities
  • People you interacted with
  • Challenges you faced
  • Successes you achieved

So What? (Feelings)

Explore your emotions and reactions to the experience

  • How did you feel during the experience?
  • What surprised or challenged you?
  • What aspects were most rewarding?
  • How did your perspectives change?

Now What? (Learning)

Identify key learnings and plan for future actions

  • What did you learn about yourself?
  • What skills did you develop?
  • How will this impact your future?
  • What would you do differently?
